Requirements

Our project stems from our client Avanade's requirements for a fast and reliable explainability application. As a company offering solutions for cloud services and AI analytics, Avanade is looking for a faster and more economic way to explain analytics to their clients. They are looking for an application that can achieve this, and is easy to deploy on systems from personal devices to large scale servers.

Project Goals

Our project goal is to develop a prototype of a product that would achieve all the requirements made by Avanade.

The primary goal for our prototype is to be able to analyse and explain machine learning models and their pairing datasets uploaded by the users. Please refer to our MoSCoW lists at the end of this page for more product goals and aims.

Requirement Gathering

Aside from our primary goal, we also gathered more requirements by communicating with our client throughout the development cycle. For example, it was required that the product should be based on Microsoft technologies as much as possible.

The team also planned a survey and gathered opinions from the general public. Only multiple choice questions were included in the survey for easier statistical analysis, but an extra "more comments" section was also included for users to suggest their own opinions and ideas. The team used Google Forms for delivering the survey as offers instant analysis of every question, saving both time and effort.

For more information on the specific requirements of our project, please refer to our MoSCoW list at the end of this page.

Personas

After gathering enough responses, the team then proceeded to create two personas and two use scenarios to model our expected users: high-level managers representing common users, and research scientists representing users with specialised knowledge in machine learning.
