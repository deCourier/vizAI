Requirements

Project Background and Client Introduction

Our project stems from one of our client, Avanade's requirements for a fast and reliable explainability module. As a corporation offering business solutions for cloud services and AI analytics, Avanade is looking for a faster and more economic way to explain analytics to their clients. The explainability module should partly be based on Microsoft technologies and services, and be easy to deploy on a variety of different systems ranging from personal devices to large scale servers.

Project Goals

The project goal is to develop a prototype of a product that would achieve all the requirements made by Avanade.

The most fundamental requirement for our prototype is to be able to analyse and explain arbitrary machine learning models and their pairing datasets uploaded by the users.

Other goals and requirements have been compiled into MoSCow lists and ranked by importance at the end of this page.

Requirement Gathering

Upon settling on the most fundamental requirements, the team set out to gather more by meeting directly with our client and surveying the general public. Only multiple choice questions were included in the survey for easier analytics. However, an extra "more comments" section was also included for users to suggest their own opinions or ideas on the planned application. The team made the choice to use Google Forms since it offers instant analysis of every question, saving both time and effort.

Personas

After gathering enough responses, the team proceeded onto creating two personas and their use scenarios to model the two expected groups of users: high-level managers and research scientists, representing the less technically advanced users and users with specialised knowledge in the area of AI and machine learning.
