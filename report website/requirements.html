Requirements

Project background and Client introduction

Our project stems from one of our client, Avanade's requirements for a fast and reliable explainability module. As a corporation offering business solutions for cloud services and AI analytics, Avanade is looking for a faster can more economic way to explain analytics to their clients. 

Project goals

The project goal is to develop a prototype of a product that would allow this to be achieved. The most fundamental requirement for our prototype is to be able to analyse and explain arbitrary machine learning models and their pairing datasets uploaded by the users.

Requirement gathering

Upon settling on the most fundamental requirement, the team set out to gather more requirements by meeting directly with our client and surveying the general public. Only multiple choice questions were included in the survey for easier analytics. However, an extra "more comments" section was also included for users to suggest their own opinions or ideas on the planned product. The team also made the choice to use Google forms since the service offers instant analysis of every question, saving both time and effort.

Personas

After gathering enough responses, the team proceeded onto creating two personas and their use scenarios to model the two expected groups of users that will be using the team's product: high-level department or group managers and research scientists, representing the less technically advanced users and users with specialised knowledge in the area of AI explainability.
